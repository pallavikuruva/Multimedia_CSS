Kyoto,Japan

Kyoto is Japan's ancient imperial capital, a living museum preserving traditional culture with thousands of temples, shrines, gardens, geisha districts, and historic wooden houses, offering a profound blend of historical heritage and modern Japanese life.

New York,USA

New York is a dynamic global hub for finance, culture, and diversity, famous for its iconic skyline, five boroughs (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, Staten Island), and endless energy, making it an influential metropolis with a vibrant mix of neighborhoods and attractions from Broadway to Central Park.

Location

Delhi,India

Delhi, India's capital, is a vibrant metropolis blending ancient history with modern life, serving as India's political, cultural, and commercial heart, famous for its rich heritage, iconic monuments like the Red Fort and Qutub Minar, diverse cuisine, and status as a major transport hub, offering a unique mix of old and new.
